Hi! I'm Bryan, and I'm a software developer.

I'm a recent Computer Science graduate from the University of San Francisco. Ever since I can remember, I have been surrounded by computers. My passion for technology came from my parents, as they are both software engineers. In middle school, I would play around with HTML, designing websites and layouts in my free time. I would go on to take my first programming course my junior year of highschool, and instantly fell in love with the idea of coding. In 2012, I enrolled in the University of San Francisco, and in May 2016, I graduated Cum Laude with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science.

USF has inspired me to enjoy tackling difficult challenges, and finding elegant solutions for them. I believe the true beauty of software development is that it requires one to think with both logic and creativity.

I am currently seeking new opportunities to develop my skill set while building bigger and better things. If you'd like to get in touch with me, feel free to contact me at barelampagos@gmail.com, or message me on my Twitter or LinkedIn (below).
